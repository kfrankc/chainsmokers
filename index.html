Chainsmokers Set Review

I took an amazing class at UCLA during Spring 2017: Music History 8 - History of Electronic Dance Music. My final assignment for the class was to analyze a DJ set, and I chose The Chainsmokers' set at Ultra Music Festival 2016.

To illustrate some of the analysis, I've created several charts throughout this esssay, focusing on bpm, drops, and mix transitions using Highcharts.js. Enjoy!
